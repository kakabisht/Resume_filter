Welcome to Resume Filter Application


My aim is to simplify the resumer filtration process, add other various parameters apart from the usual resume fields such as voice sample which will include speech mood, articulation-rate, speech rate and number of filler words.

  1. Applicants can Sign up, log in and fill the resume application form of companies.
  2. Recruiters can further view the resume application form data and sort them according to soft skills or hard skills or a combination of both.
